Welcome and Overview

Welcome to the experiment! In this study, you will make choices between different lottery options. Each choice involves probabilities, rewards, and time delays. This is a preference task, so there are no right or wrong answers. We are only interested in which option you prefer the most.

Understanding Lottery Options

Each lottery option has three key components:

  • Rewards: The amount you can win if the lottery is successful.
  • Probabilities: The chance of winning the reward.
  • Time delays: How long you might need to wait for the outcome.

For example, you might see an option like: "50% chance to win $500 after 5 weeks"
